Christmas Day Lunch

Come and join us for this special day

Christmas Day Lunch at Vilagrad Winery

Come and join us for this special Christmas Day lunch. The children will be entertained with Santa Claus, treasure hunts and lollie scrambles. This day will be a memorable occasion enjoy easy listening music and Christmas carols. while the sun shines on the vines relax in our courtyards or even roam through the vines with a glass of Vilagrad wine.

 

Adults $98 and Children $45 (surcharge included)                                  

Book Now

Day Menu

Open 12pm to 4pm -  buffet lunch at 1pm 

Mains

  • Whole spit roast pork with apple sauce- gluten and dairy free
  • Roast chicken with herb and cranberry stuffing- stuffing contains gluten
  • Hot glazed ham on the bone with pineapple and glazed cherries- gluten and dairy free
  • Fresh steamed snapper & mussels with sautéed shanghai served with a reduced white wine and coconut cream sauce- gluten and dairy free
  • Roasted sirloin with a beef glaze and horseradish grain mustard- gluten and dairy free

Salad and Vegetables

  • Gourmet roasted agria potatoes with garlic and parmesan cheese- gluten free
  • Fresh steamed garden vegetables in a Croatian roast red capsicum sauce- gluten and dairy free
  • Green leafy salad with tomato, avocado, radish, snow peas, cucumber and Vilagrad French dressing- gluten and dairy free
  • Spicy nut and crispy parsnip salad with vinaigrette- gluten and dairy free
  • Selection of homemade relishes and chutneys- gluten and dairy free

Dessert

  • Christmas fruit steam pudding with brandy custard
  • Hazelnut meringue nest filled with a lemon citrus mousse- gluten free
  • Chocolate cake filled with a port, blueberry and cream ganache
  • Caramel butterscotch sauce, natural yoghurt, fresh whipped cream and black doris plums- gluten free

Tea/Coffee

  • Mount Pirongia origin roasted coffee
  • Earl Grey, and a selection of tropical & herbal teas

Accompanied by fresh milk, coffee crystal & sugar

Vilagrad reserves the right to menu changes if required.
